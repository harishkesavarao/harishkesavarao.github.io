I build the data infrastructure that powers AI applications in production — lakehouse architecture, embedding pipelines, vector storage, and large-scale Spark data platforms.
Over 12 years at Atlassian, Databricks, Amazon, Salesforce, and Indeed, I have shipped systems handling petabyte-scale data across AWS and Azure. My work sits at the intersection of data engineering and AI/ML enablement — from high-throughput ingestion to RAG frameworks, semantic search, and LLM data pipelines.
I am an active open-source contributor to Apache Airflow, Delta Lake, and DataHub, and I write about data engineering and AI infrastructure on this site.
